# Code of conduct

AgentKeys welcomes contributors with different backgrounds, identities, experience levels, and perspectives.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and constructive.
- Discuss the work rather than attacking the person doing it.
- Make space for questions and good-faith mistakes.
- Protect credentials, private prompts, and other sensitive information.
- Treat safety and permission-boundary concerns as engineering input, not obstacles to dismiss.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and sustained bad-faith disruption are not tolerated in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the contact method on the maintainer's GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, limit participation, or ban accounts when necessary to protect the community.

Maintainers will handle reports as confidentially as practical and avoid conflicts of interest when reviewing them.
